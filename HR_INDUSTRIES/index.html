Nuts and bolts

Nuts and bolts are essential fastening hardware used in a wide range of applications, typically to hold components together securely. Here's a breakdown of each:

Metal bolts 3D

A metal bolt is a cylindrical fastening device with external threads along a portion or the entirety of its shaft, designed to work with a nut to clamp two or more objects together.

Metal Nuts

Metal nuts are fastening components with internal threading that pair with bolts to secure materials together. They come in a variety of shapes, sizes, and materials, designed for different mechanical, construction, and industrial applications
